Our Campus

Our team is from Allen High School which is located in Allen, TX. However our robotics club works out of the STEAM Center building. The STEAM Center was built by our district in order to provide more opportunities for STEAM based careers. There is a complete wood and metal workshop, computer labs, a makerspace, and much more inside this amazing building.

Introducing BEST to Our School

As previously mentioned, the STEAM Center provides science, technology, engineering, art, and mathematic opportunities. One class offered at this campus is Engineering Design and Presentation. We have introduced the BEST competition to these students to encourage them to stay in STEAM classes and to interest them in BEST and robotics. After showing them the game, they were assigned a project to create parts of a robot that would fit the tasks provided by the game. They followed the engineering design process just like us.
